Pixal started out as a small company working to develop cameras that would take high quality pictures regardless of light conditions. Our loyal employees started out working in a small office over a deli in Manhattan.

We gained in popularity due to the invention of our own unique software that can edit any image format found on the web today. Over the years we have worked to make it user-friendly. Although it has many of the functions necessary to edit professional photographs, each function is easy to learn.
Our built-in tutorial also shows the user how each tool works and what types of things it can be used for. This is all due to the hard work of every employee in our corporation. When you buy from Pixal, each of us stand behind you, as the user. Let us be your tools so that you can show the world what you see when you look through the lense!
